About Us

About Yassin Group

Leading company specialized in the manufacturing of hookah glass. Established to meet both local and global market demands in the hookah industry, we offer high-quality products featuring modern technology and innovative designs that cater to a variety of tastes.

Our Vision

We aim to be the world leader in the hookah glass manufacturing industry, focusing on innovation and quality in every aspect of our work.

Our Mission

We strive to be the ideal partner for our customers in the hookah industry, using the latest technologies and high-quality materials to ensure product durability and performance.

Our History

Yassin Group was founded in 2010 with the goal of becoming a leader in the hookah glass industry. From the very beginning, we focused on the finer details of design and manufacturing quality.
